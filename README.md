## About Me: 
I'm an aspiring mathematician with an unrelenting curiosity for the beauty hidden in numbers, patterns, and structures. My passion lies in taking the chaos of everyday life and translating it into elegant mathematical models — because if you look closely enough, everything around us is just math waiting to be discovered.

> *"The universe is an equation — I'm just solving for the unknown."*
